# Proposal 0004 - Atmosphere Commons

Status: Draft

Preview URL:

- `/?proposal=atmosphere-commons`

## Why this exists

This proposal explores how the app could become more connective, joyful, and environmentally useful without losing the joke.

It focuses on:

- shared humanity
- positive world rituals
- optional acts of repair
- gentle environmental awareness

## What Claude should review

1. Does this expand the emotional range of the app in a good way?
2. Which parts feel real and which parts feel too sentimental?
3. Should “Acts of Repair” live in the main shell, a dedicated mode, or an occasional panel?
4. Which pieces are worth prototyping first: rituals, postcards, repair actions, or eco mode?

## What is intentionally fake in this draft

- impact metrics
- missions
- postcards
- action partners

## Expected merge targets if accepted

- `src/components/HUD/`
- `src/App.jsx`
- `src/styles/`
- `server/` if real mission or impact data is added later

## Notes

This proposal is trying to answer: how can the app stay hilarious while also nudging people toward kindness, curiosity, and care?
